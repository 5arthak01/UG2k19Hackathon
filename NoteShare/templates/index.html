Welcome to NoteShare!



NoteShare is a notes sharing and QP archive web-app. Allows images, text files, archives and PDFs as uploads and stores them. A simple, easy-to-use solution to repetitive, redundant requests for notes and previous year papers on whatsapp groups.


Flask based back-end and powered by JavaScript at front-end. Bootstrap used with HTML5 for beautiful, responsive UI.
